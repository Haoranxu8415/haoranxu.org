About Me

I have a background in design with a strong passion for UI/UX. My focus lies in creating intuitive and engaging digital experiences that connect users with technology seamlessly.

Future Goals

My goal is to build digital experiences that feel as seamless and alive as the physical world — interfaces that breathe, adapt, and empower. I’m especially interested in how ethical design, AI, and behavioral psychology intersect to shape how we engage with technology. Long-term, I hope to study business and design at a global university, then lead or join a team creating tools that help people think, learn, and express themselves better.
