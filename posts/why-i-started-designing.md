
It started with something a classmate said: go register a domain in your own name, even if you just leave it there as a placeholder.

I did not think too much about it at the time. But the idea landed. In the summer of 2025, I bought the domain and started building. My frontend skills were limited, what came out was rough, but I knew what I wanted this to become.

I am not a student with particularly impressive standardised test scores. For a long time, I was aware that other people's understanding of me was incomplete. They saw certain numbers and missed everything else. But that everything else was precisely what I cared most about: what I had done, why I had done it, how I thought about things. There was nowhere to put any of that.

I have always felt that a person's life is a story. Famous or forgotten, I want the one who writes it down to be me. Not a summary written by someone else. Not a resume that reduces everything to bullet points. My own words, about the things I actually lived through.

This website is that place. It holds everything I want to say to the world: the photographs I took, the projects I built, the thoughts I wrote down. Incomplete, still growing, but every part of it is something I chose to put here.
