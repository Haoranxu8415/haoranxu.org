Algorithm Mirror

An interactive web-based prototype that simulates the inner workings of social media recommendation algorithms. Users swipe through short-form video content, with their interactions — likes, skips, dwell time, interest selections — logged and analysed in real time. The system generates a personalised summary at session end, visualising the algorithm's "understanding" of the user's preferences.

The project explores how seemingly neutral data tracking influences self-perception, highlighting the psychological and ethical dimensions of algorithmic personalisation. Built with HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript; UI reflects a minimalist aesthetic aiming to be both elegant and immersive.
